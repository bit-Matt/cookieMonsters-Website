About Us

Meet the Cookie Monsters!


A group of college students that aspire to enhance their programming skills in creating various types of projects. The group name was inspired by the term "cookies." "Cookies" are used to help remember information about the user's visit to the website, which can both make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to the user. Each "Cookie" of this jar has been specifically chosen for the unique set of skills.
